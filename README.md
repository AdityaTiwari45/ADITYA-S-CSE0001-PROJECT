## TASK 1 REPORT

For this project, I used the digital design tool Canva to create an infographic on the topic of digital literacy. This tool helped me organize my ideas visually using shapes, colors, and text boxes, making the content easy to understand and visually appealing.

My design presents the concept of digital literacy in a structured manner. It includes the definition of digital literacy, essential skills required (such as using computers, searching for information, and effective communication), useful digital tools for students (including online learning platforms and coding tools), and important safety practices like creating strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links.

The infographic is designed in such a way that all sections are connected to the central theme, making it clear and informative. One challenge I faced was arranging all the information neatly without making the design look crowded. It required careful word selection and proper spacing. However, I found the process interesting as it allowed me to creatively present information visually instead of using plain text. This project helped me improve both my understanding of digital literacy and my design skills.
